About Us

Started in 2018, Our company mission is to provide highly experienced Project Management professionals on a flexible and immediate basis. Loch Technologies was formed from years of experience in the consulting engineering market and after decades of Project and Construction management support globally.

Loch Technologies provides highly skilled and experienced Project management professionals to Clients that have a need for self-motivated professional support that will provide an immediate contribution to their Projects and planning. We accomplish this by managing and vetting qualifications of our support group in coordination with our Client’s scope of work. This process and focus set us apart from our competition who may only provide candidates or support based on availability, cv data-match, or economic/rate considerations. We focus our proposed services on the needs of the Client and will assist our client’s in clearly defining their needs through our proprietary application called “Done”. Done provides our clients with a simple means of defining their scope and identifying the key critical components to their project support needs.

We work on behalf of Clients and support their businesses as though they were our own. Our support and mission is to focus on our Client’s success and deliver services that ensure our clients recognize our respect, trust, commitment, and ownership of their project support needs.
